(一) namo ratna trayaya  namo arya  valokite svaraya  

bodhisattvaya mahasattvaya   maha karunikaya  

om satvaya bhayesu tranam karaya taasya                                    

namas krtva imam arya valokite - svara tva



nila kantha namo hrdayam avarta yisyami sarvartha sadhnam subham ajeyam

bhava marga visudhakam

(二)tadyatha om aloke aloka mati aloka atikrante hehe hare  maha bodhi sattva smara smara hrdayam kuru dkuru kramam

sadhaya sadhaya dhuru dhuru viyanti maha viyanti

dhara dhara dharidre svara cala cala avimala  vimala avimala murte ehyeh

lokesvara raga visam vinasaya dvesa visam vinasaya

moha cala visam vinasaya    hulu hulu mala hulu hulu hare padma nabha  

sara sara siri siri suru suru      budhaya budhaya bodhaya bodhaya
(三)  maitreya nila kantha  kamasya darsanam prahradaya nanam  svaha      

siddhaya svaha maha siddhaya svaha    siddha yoge svaraya svaha
       nila kantha svaha    waraha mukha simha mukhaya svaha
     

padma hastaya svaha   cakra yuddhaya svaha

sankha sadane bodhanaya svaha    maha lakuta dharaya  svaha
   vama skandha disa sthita krsna jinaya  svaha  

vyaghra carma nivasanaya svaha

(四) namo ratna trayaya mano arya  valokite svaraya svaha

om sidhyantu mantra padaya svaha
